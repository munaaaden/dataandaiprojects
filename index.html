About me

I am a public health practitioner with a proven track record of spearheading impactful initiatives, proactively identifying health needs, and translating data into action. My experience spans public health emergency operations, epidemiology and surveillance, advanced analytics (R, Python, QGIS, Power BI, STATA, DHIS2), and collaborative research. I contribute to outbreak intelligence, policy translation, and capacity building for Nairobi’s health ecosystem.
